Welcome!

Hey there! My name is Jonathan Fuentes. I am a proud husband, father of two wonderful kids, and dedicated family man who finds joy in fishing, spending quality time with loved ones, and caring for my dogs. At 29, I have built a life around passion and hard work, running multiple businesses in property management, Airbnb accommodations and cleaning, and American Bully breeding. Currently, I am pursuing my Bachelor of Science in Software Development, with plans to graduate in October 2027. Whether it is on the water with a fishing rod or in front of my laptop coding, I am striving to build a meaningful future for my family and myself.
